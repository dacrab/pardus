# PARDUS

A natural wine project from the western slopes of a forgotten Aegean island.

Low intervention. High altitude. Single-vineyard.

We harvest by hand, ferment in old clay and concrete, and bottle unfined and unfiltered. The vineyard is the recipe. The vintage is the chef. We are, at best, the kitchen porter who does not get in the way.

---

Six hundred and twelve metres above a sea that does not forgive.

**Soil** — Schist, limestone, decomposed clay
**Aspect** — North-west, exposed to the meltemi
**Vines** — Avg. 67 years, massale selection
**Varietals** — Assyrtiko, Malagousia, Mavrotragano
